Our Audit and Assurance services can be benchmarked with the highest degree of Integrity and Independence. We provide financial insights into the business areas that are left unnoticed, encouraging trust and confidence.

Audit Types

Comprehensive Audit Services

Statutory Audit

+

Audit of accounts to review the accuracy of a company's financial statements and records to determine a true and fair view in accordance with Companies Act, 2013 and Income Tax Act, 1961.

Internal Audit

+

Evaluation of internal controls, risk management systems, and governance processes to enhance operational efficiency and ensure compliance.

Forensic Audit

+

Investigation of financial irregularities, fraud detection, and evidence collection for legal proceedings and dispute resolution.

Management Audit

+

Assessment of management decisions, strategies, and performance to improve effectiveness and organizational outcomes.

Concurrent Audit

+

Real-time examination of transactions to ensure compliance with internal policies and regulatory requirements.

Due Diligence Audit

+

Comprehensive financial and legal evaluation conducted during mergers, acquisitions, or investments.

Operational Audit

+

Analysis of business operations and processes to improve productivity and efficiency.

Compliance Audit

+

Verification of adherence to statutory laws, regulations, and internal policies.
